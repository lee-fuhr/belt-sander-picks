How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or timed trials. Instead, we dig into what real owners say after months and years of use, and we cross‑reference those experiences with expert reviews from reputable woodworking and tool publications. We look for patterns: which sanders break down, which have frustrating belt tracking, and which manage dust well enough to keep your shop livable. Long‑term reliability matters most. A sander that feels great in the first hour can develop vibration issues or belt misalignment after a few projects. By tracking owner feedback over time and noting common failure points, switches, platen wear, tracking knobs, we build a honest picture of what will serve you well beyond the first few sheets of sandpaper.
